### CSM Job Description

**Title:** Client Success Manager
**Reports to:** Founder/CEO
**Salary Range:** $60,000 - $80,000 + bonus

**Responsibilities:**
- Own client relationships post-sale
- Manage onboarding process (with automation support)
- Conduct bi-weekly strategy calls
- Monitor campaign performance daily
- Identify upsell/expansion opportunities
- Handle support inquiries
- Collect testimonials and referrals

**Requirements:**
- 2-4 years in customer success or account management
- B2B SaaS or agency experience preferred
- Strong communication skills
- Data-driven mindset
- Comfortable with automation tools

**Success Metrics:**
- Client retention >90%
- NPS >50
- Response time <4 hours
- Expansion rate >10%

### AE Job Description

**Title:** Account Executive
**Reports to:** Founder/CEO
**Salary Range:** $70,000 - $100,000 base + commission

**Responsibilities:**
- Own outbound prospecting (using XGrowthOS)
- Conduct discovery calls
- Present proposals and close deals
- Manage sales pipeline
- Meet monthly quota
- Participate in marketing initiatives

**Requirements:**
- 3-5 years B2B sales experience
- Proven track record of quota attainment
- Experience selling services or SaaS
- Self-motivated, organized
- CRM proficiency

**Compensation:**
- Base: $70-100K
- Commission: 10% of Year 1 contract value
- OTE: $120-150K

**Success Metrics:**
- New clients: 5+/month
- Discovery calls: 20+/month
- Win rate: 25%+
- Average deal size: growing

## Compensation Philosophy

### Principles

1. **Competitive:** Pay at or above market for role
2. **Performance-aligned:** Significant variable component
3. **Equity participation:** Key hires get equity
4. **Transparent:** Clear comp structures
5. **Growth-oriented:** Raises tied to company and individual growth

## Key Takeaways

1. **Start solo:** Founder handles everything until 10 clients
2. **CSM first:** Client success is the first hire
3. **Sales second:** Add AE when capacity allows
4. **Hire for capacity:** Wait until needed, not before
5. **Compensation matters:** Pay well, align incentives
6. **Equity for key hires:** Build ownership culture
