## Key Metrics Dashboard

### Weekly Tracking

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Bookings | New contracts signed | 1+/week |
| Pipeline | Discovery calls scheduled | 3+/week |
| MRR | Sum of active retainers + avg meetings | Growing |

### Monthly Tracking

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| New MRR | Revenue from new clients | Growing |
| Expansion MRR | Upsells from existing | 10% of MRR |
| Churned MRR | Lost revenue from churn | <5% of MRR |
| Net New MRR | New + Expansion - Churn | Positive |
| Gross Revenue Retention | (Start MRR - Churn) / Start MRR | >90% |
| Net Revenue Retention | (Start + Expansion - Churn) / Start | >100% |

## Forecasting Process

### Monthly Review

1. Compare actual vs forecast
2. Identify variance drivers
3. Update assumptions
4. Revise forward forecast
5. Document learnings

### Quarterly Deep Dive

1. Cohort performance analysis
2. Churn reason analysis
3. Expansion opportunity assessment
4. Market/competitive review
5. Annual plan revision

## Key Takeaways

1. **Base case Year 1: ~$1M revenue** with 34 clients
2. **Churn is the biggest lever** - 3% vs 8% = $400K difference
3. **Meeting bonuses are 50%+ of revenue** - performance matters
4. **Break-even is fast** - <1 client covers fixed costs
5. **Cash flow positive from Day 1** - no funding required
6. **Year 2 acceleration** - $3.7M revenue at base case
