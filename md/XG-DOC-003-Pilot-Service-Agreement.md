## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1** "Services" means the B2B lead generation services described in Section 2.

**1.2** "Qualified Meeting" means a scheduled meeting between Client and a Prospect where: (a) the Prospect matches Client's ICP as defined in the Discovery Questionnaire; (b) the meeting is confirmed and takes place; (c) the Prospect has decision-making authority or influence.

**1.3** "Prospect" means a potential customer contacted through the Services.

**1.4** "ICP" means Ideal Customer Profile as defined in the Discovery Questionnaire.

**1.5** "Pilot Period" means the initial 90-day term of this Agreement.

**1.6** "AI Engines" means the five automated systems: The Guardian, The Architect, The Scientist, The Hunter, and The Sentinel.

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 2.1 Core Services

Provider shall deliver the following services during the Pilot Period:

**a) Campaign Development & Management**
- ICP definition and targeting strategy
- Email copy creation via The Architect
- Multi-sequence campaign design
- A/B testing and optimization
- Campaign monitoring and adjustments

**b) Compliance & Deliverability (The Guardian)**
- DNC (Do-Not-Contact) verification
- Domain health monitoring
- SPF/DKIM/DMARC verification
- Blacklist monitoring
- Spam complaint management

**c) Lead Qualification & Delivery**
- Prospect identification matching ICP
- Email outreach execution
- Reply management and qualification
- Meeting scheduling coordination
- CRM integration and lead delivery

**d) Optimization & Intelligence**
- The Scientist: A/B testing and optimization
- The Hunter: Reply-based lead expansion
- The Sentinel: Website visitor intelligence (if enabled)

**e) Reporting & Analytics**
- Weekly performance reports
- Real-time dashboard access
- Monthly strategy calls
- Quarterly business reviews

### 2.2 Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:
- Complete Discovery Questionnaire within 48 hours
- Provide CRM access for lead delivery
- Provide customer/competitor exclusion lists
- Respond to copy approval requests within 2 business days
- Attend scheduled sync calls
- Provide timely feedback on lead quality
- Maintain accurate meeting attribution in CRM

## 4. TERM & TERMINATION

### 4.1 Initial Term

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for an initial Pilot Period of ninety (90) days.

### 4.2 Renewal

After the Pilot Period, this Agreement automatically renews on a month-to-month basis unless either party provides thirty (30) days written notice of termination.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party:
- Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 15 days of notice
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Engages in illegal or unethical conduct

### 4.4 Termination for Performance

If reply rate remains below 3% for thirty (30) consecutive days despite good-faith optimization efforts, Client may terminate with 15 days notice and receive a pro-rated refund of unused retainer.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- Provider will complete work-in-progress and deliver final reports
- Client will pay all outstanding invoices within 15 days
- Provider will export and transfer all lead data to Client
- Confidentiality obligations survive termination

---

## 5. PERFORMANCE GUARANTEES

### 5.1 Performance Floor

Provider guarantees minimum performance standards:
- Reply Rate: 3% minimum after 30-day ramp period
- Deliverability: 95% minimum inbox placement
- Bounce Rate: Below 3%
- Spam Complaints: Below 0.1%

### 5.2 Remediation Process

If performance falls below guaranteed minimums:
1. Provider will notify Client within 24 hours
2. Provider will implement remediation plan within 48 hours
3. If not resolved within 30 days, Client may invoke Section 4.4

### 5.3 Service Credits

| Issue | Credit |
|-------|--------|
| P1 Response > 2 hours | 5% of monthly retainer |
| System uptime < 99.5% | 5% of monthly retainer |
| Reply rate < 3% for 30+ days | 10% of monthly retainer |
| Maximum monthly credits | 25% of monthly retainer |

## 6. MEETING ATTRIBUTION

### 6.1 Attribution Method

Meetings are attributed to Provider when:
- The Prospect was first contacted through Provider's campaigns
- The meeting is logged in Client's CRM with Provider's campaign source
- The meeting takes place (not canceled/no-show)

### 6.2 Attribution Window

Provider receives attribution credit for meetings booked within 90 days of initial outreach contact.

### 6.3 CRM Access

Client shall provide read-only CRM access to verify meeting attribution. Provider may audit attribution quarterly.

### 6.4 Attribution Disputes

Disputes regarding meeting attribution shall be resolved in good faith. If unable to resolve within 10 business days, parties agree to binding arbitration per Section 11.
