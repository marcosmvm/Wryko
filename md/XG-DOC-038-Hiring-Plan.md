## Job Descriptions

### Client Success Manager

**About XGrowthOS:**
XGrowthOS is an autonomous B2B lead generation platform powered by 11 AI engines. We help companies book qualified meetings through AI-powered cold email, then deliver exceptional service through our CSM Automation Suite.

**The Role:**
As our first Client Success Manager, you'll own the client relationship post-sale. You'll ensure clients achieve their goals, identify expansion opportunities, and build the foundation for our CS function.

**Responsibilities:**
- Own onboarding for new clients (supported by automation)
- Conduct bi-weekly strategy calls with clients
- Monitor campaign performance and identify optimization opportunities
- Handle client support inquiries with fast, thoughtful responses
- Identify upsell and expansion opportunities
- Collect testimonials, referrals, and case studies
- Collaborate with founder on product feedback and improvements

**Requirements:**
- 2-4 years in customer success, account management, or similar role
- B2B SaaS or agency experience strongly preferred
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Data-driven mindset - comfortable with metrics and reporting
- Self-starter who thrives with autonomy
- Experience with CRM and CS tools

**Nice to Have:**
- Experience with cold email or lead generation
- Technical aptitude (APIs, automation tools)
- Previous startup experience

**What We Offer:**
- Competitive salary ($60-80K) + performance bonus
- Equity stake (0.25-0.5%)
- Remote-first culture
- Ground-floor opportunity at fast-growing startup
- Work directly with founder

## Interview Process

### CSM Interview Process (4 stages, 2 weeks)

**Stage 1: Application Review (1-2 days)**
- Resume and cover letter review
- Initial screen for requirements
- Decision: Advance / Reject

**Stage 2: Phone Screen (30 min)**
- Basic fit assessment
- Motivation and background
- Salary expectations
- Key questions:
  - "Why customer success?"
  - "Tell me about a difficult client situation."
  - "What tools have you used?"
- Decision: Advance / Reject

**Stage 3: Hiring Manager Interview (60 min)**
- Deep dive on experience
- Role-play: Client call simulation
- Case study: "Client is underperforming - what do you do?"
- Cultural fit discussion
- Questions from candidate

**Stage 4: Work Sample + Final (90 min)**
- Task: Write an email to a frustrated client
- Task: Review mock dashboard and identify issues
- Discussion of work sample
- Logistics, compensation, timing
- Two-way Q&A

**Decision Meeting:**
- Same day or next day decision
- Reference checks (2 professional)
- Offer extended

---

### AE Interview Process (5 stages, 3 weeks)

**Stage 1: Application Review**

**Stage 2: Phone Screen (30 min)**
- Sales background and metrics
- Deal examples
- Motivation for XGrowthOS

**Stage 3: Sales Skill Interview (60 min)**
- Deep dive on deals won and lost
- Discovery skill assessment
- Objection handling scenarios
- Pipeline management approach

**Stage 4: Mock Discovery Call (45 min)**
- Role-play: Candidate runs discovery
- Interviewer plays prospect
- Debrief and feedback

**Stage 5: Founder Interview + Close (60 min)**
- Cultural fit
- Compensation negotiation
- Start date and logistics
