## Terms & Conditions

### Eligibility

- Active XGrowthOS clients in good standing
- Referral must be a new prospect (not in our pipeline)
- One credit per unique company/referral
- Self-referrals not eligible

### Credit Application

- Credits applied to monthly retainer invoices
- Credits do not apply to onboarding fees
- Credits do not expire while you're an active client
- Credits are non-transferable and non-cashable
- Maximum credit per invoice: 100% of retainer

### Disqualifications

A referral does not qualify if:
- They're already in our sales pipeline
- They've previously had a discovery call with us
- They don't meet our ICP criteria
- They provide false contact information
- The introduction was not from you directly

### Disputes

Any disputes regarding referral credit will be resolved in good faith. XGrowthOS reserves the right to make final determinations on referral qualification.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When do I receive my credit?

Credits are applied to your next invoice after the referral completes their discovery call.

### Can I earn multiple credits?

Yes! There's no limit to how many referrals you can make. Each qualified referral = $500 credit.

### What if my referral doesn't become a client?

You still receive the credit! They just need to complete a discovery call.

### Can I refer someone I barely know?

We recommend warm introductions. Cold referrals rarely result in discovery calls and won't qualify.

### What if someone I referred comes back later?

If they complete a discovery call within 90 days of your referral, you receive credit. After 90 days, the referral expires.

### Can I refer another company I own?

No, self-referrals (including companies you own or are a major stakeholder in) are not eligible.
