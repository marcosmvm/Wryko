## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Guidelines

| Type | Examples | Open Rate Target |
|------|----------|------------------|
| **Personalized** | "{{first_name}}, quick question" | 50%+ |
| **Curiosity** | "Something I noticed about {{company}}" | 45%+ |
| **Direct** | "Outbound help for {{company}}" | 40%+ |
| **Social Proof** | "How {{similar_company}} books 12 meetings/month" | 45%+ |

### Formatting Rules

- Keep emails under 150 words
- Use short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- One clear CTA per email
- Mobile-friendly (no wide tables)
- Plain text preferred over HTML

### Timing Optimization

| Day | Best Send Time | Notes |
|-----|----------------|-------|
| Tuesday | 9-10 AM | Highest engagement |
| Wednesday | 9-10 AM | Second best |
| Thursday | 9-10 AM | Good for follow-ups |
| Monday | 11 AM | After morning meetings |
| Friday | Avoid | Low engagement |

## Tracking & Optimization

### Metrics to Monitor

### A/B Testing Plan

Test one element at a time:
1. Subject lines (week 1)
2. Opening hooks (week 2)
3. CTAs (week 3)
4. Send times (week 4)
