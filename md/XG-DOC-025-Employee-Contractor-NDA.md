## 1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

### 1.1 Confidential Information Includes

"Confidential Information" means any and all information disclosed to or learned by Recipient, whether oral, written, electronic, or visual, including but not limited to:

**Business Information:**
- Business plans, strategies, and projections
- Financial data, pricing, and commercial terms
- Marketing plans and materials
- Client lists and prospect data
- Partnership and vendor relationships
- Internal processes and procedures

**Client Information:**
- Client names and contact information
- Client data and lead lists
- Campaign performance data
- Client business information
- Any information received from clients

**Technical Information:**
- AI engine algorithms and logic
- Software code and architecture
- Automation workflows (n8n)
- API integrations and configurations
- Master Library content and templates
- System designs and specifications

**Proprietary Methods:**
- The Guardian, Architect, Scientist, Hunter, Sentinel engines
- Optimization methodologies
- Compliance checking processes
- Lead expansion techniques
- Performance benchmarks

### 1.2 Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of Recipient;

(b) Was lawfully in Recipient's possession prior to disclosure, as documented;

(c) Is independently developed by Recipient without use of Confidential Information;

(d) Is received from a third party with lawful right to disclose;

(e) Is disclosed with Company's prior written consent;

(f) Is required to be disclosed by law, provided Recipient gives prompt notice to Company.

---

## 2. OBLIGATIONS OF RECIPIENT

### 2.1 Non-Disclosure

Recipient agrees to:

(a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;

(b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party;

(c) Not discuss Confidential Information in public places or unsecured communications;

(d) Not post Confidential Information online or on social media;

(e) Immediately notify Company of any suspected unauthorized disclosure.

### 2.2 Limited Use

Recipient agrees to:

(a) Use Confidential Information solely for performing duties for Company;

(b) Not use Confidential Information for personal benefit;

(c) Not use Confidential Information to compete with Company;

(d) Not use Confidential Information to solicit Company's clients.

### 2.3 Protection Standard

Recipient agrees to:

(a) Protect Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care used to protect own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care;

(b) Store Confidential Information securely;

(c) Use only approved devices and systems for accessing Confidential Information;

(d) Not copy Confidential Information except as necessary for duties;

(e) Not remove Confidential Information from Company premises or systems without authorization.

### 2.4 Security Practices

Recipient agrees to:

(a) Use strong, unique passwords for all Company accounts;

(b) Enable multi-factor authentication where available;

(c) Not share login credentials with anyone;

(d) Lock devices when unattended;

(e) Not access Company systems from unsecured networks;

(f) Report security incidents immediately.

---

## 3. CLIENT DATA OBLIGATIONS

### 3.1 Special Protection

Recipient acknowledges that client data requires heightened protection and agrees to:

(a) Access client data only as necessary for assigned duties;

(b) Never export or copy client data for personal retention;

(c) Never contact clients directly without authorization;

(d) Report any client data breaches immediately;

(e) Comply with all applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM).

### 3.2 No Retention

Upon termination of engagement, Recipient agrees to:

(a) Return or delete all client data;

(b) Not retain copies of client data in any form;

(c) Certify in writing that all client data has been returned or destroyed.

---

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 4.1 Company Ownership

Recipient acknowledges and agrees that:

(a) All Confidential Information is and remains Company's property;

(b) All work product created during engagement belongs to Company;

(c) Any improvements to Company's systems or methods belong to Company;

(d) Recipient has no ownership rights in Company intellectual property.

### 4.2 Work for Hire

Any work product created by Recipient in the course of engagement is "work for hire" under U.S. copyright law. To the extent any work product does not qualify as work for hire, Recipient hereby assigns all rights to Company.

### 4.3 Moral Rights Waiver

Recipient waives any moral rights in work product to the extent permitted by law.

---

## 5. NON-SOLICITATION

### 5.1 Client Non-Solicitation

During engagement and for twelve (12) months after termination, Recipient agrees not to:

(a) Directly or indirectly solicit Company's clients;

(b) Provide competing services to Company's clients;

(c) Divert business opportunities from Company;

(d) Interfere with Company's client relationships.

### 5.2 Employee Non-Solicitation

During engagement and for twelve (12) months after termination, Recipient agrees not to:

(a) Solicit Company's employees or contractors;

(b) Encourage others to leave Company;

(c) Hire Company's personnel for competing ventures.
