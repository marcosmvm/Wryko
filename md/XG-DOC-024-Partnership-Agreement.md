## 2. PARTNERSHIP TYPES & TERMS

### 2.1 Referral Partnership

**Program Overview:**
Partner refers qualified leads to XGrowthOS and receives compensation for converted clients.

**Compensation:**

| Tier | Annual Referrals | Commission |
|------|------------------|------------|
| Bronze | 1-5 | 10% of first 6 months retainer |
| Silver | 6-15 | 15% of first 12 months retainer |
| Gold | 16+ | 20% of first 12 months retainer |

**Qualification Criteria:**
- Referral must complete discovery call
- Referral must sign pilot agreement
- Referral must not be existing XGrowthOS prospect
- Referral source must be documented

**Payment Terms:**
- Commission paid monthly
- Net 30 from client payment receipt
- Via ACH or PayPal

### 2.2 Reseller Partnership

**Program Overview:**
Partner purchases XGrowthOS services at wholesale and resells to own clients.

**Pricing:**

| Service | Wholesale Price | Suggested Resale |
|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Onboarding | $1,750 | $3,000+ |
| Monthly Retainer | $1,400/mo | $2,500+/mo |
| Per Meeting | $175 | $300+ |

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 3 clients to activate
- Quarterly minimums may apply
- Partner handles client relationship
- XGrowthOS provides fulfillment

### 2.3 Technology Partnership

**Integration Types:**

| Integration | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| API Access | Access to XGrowthOS APIs |
| Webhook Integration | Event-driven data sharing |
| CRM Connector | Native integration with partner CRM |
| Co-built Features | Joint product development |

**Technical Requirements:**
- API documentation provided
- Sandbox environment available
- Support during integration
- Security review required

### 2.4 Co-Marketing Partnership

**Activities May Include:**
- Joint webinars
- Co-branded content
- Shared case studies
- Event sponsorship
- Newsletter features

**Cost Sharing:**
- Typically 50/50 split
- Case-by-case negotiation
- Lead sharing terms defined per campaign

### 2.5 White Label Partnership

**Program Overview:**
Partner offers XGrowthOS services under their own brand.

**Terms:**
- Custom branding on dashboard
- Partner-branded emails
- Dedicated infrastructure
- Minimum volume commitments
- Custom pricing based on volume

**Pricing:** Custom quote required

## 3. PARTNER OBLIGATIONS

### 3.1 General Obligations

Partner agrees to:
- Represent XGrowthOS services accurately
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Maintain confidentiality of XGrowthOS information
- Not make unauthorized commitments on behalf of XGrowthOS
- Provide accurate reporting of referrals/sales
- Maintain professional reputation

### 3.2 Marketing Guidelines

Partner may:
- Use approved XGrowthOS marketing materials
- Reference XGrowthOS partnership in marketing (with approval)
- Share XGrowthOS content with attribution

Partner may not:
- Modify XGrowthOS branding without approval
- Make claims not supported by XGrowthOS
- Bid on XGrowthOS brand terms in paid search
- Represent themselves as XGrowthOS employees

### 3.3 Training

Partner agrees to:
- Complete initial partnership training
- Attend quarterly update sessions
- Stay current on XGrowthOS offerings

## 9. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 9.1 Initial Term

This Agreement is effective for an initial term of one (1) year from the Effective Date.

### 9.2 Renewal

This Agreement automatically renews for successive one (1) year terms unless either party provides sixty (60) days written notice of non-renewal.

### 9.3 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate with sixty (60) days written notice.

### 9.4 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate immediately if the other party:
- Materially breaches and fails to cure within 30 days
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Engages in illegal or unethical conduct
- Causes material harm to the other party's reputation

### 9.5 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- Partner ceases use of XGrowthOS materials
- Outstanding commissions paid within 30 days
- Confidentiality obligations continue
- Customer relationships handled per wind-down plan
