## Key Takeaways

1. **Consistency > Volume:** Better to post 3x/week consistently than 10x one week and 0 the next
2. **Original POV matters:** Share actual insights, not generic advice
3. **Data is compelling:** Use numbers and benchmarks
4. **Repurpose everything:** One blog post = 5 LinkedIn posts = 1 newsletter
5. **Engage, don't broadcast:** Respond to comments, start conversations
