## 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship concerning B2B lead generation services ("Purpose"). In connection with this Purpose, each Party may disclose to the other certain confidential and proprietary information. The Parties agree to protect such information as set forth below.

---

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

### 2.1 Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any and all information or data disclosed by one Party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other Party (the "Receiving Party"), whether orally, in writing, or by any other means, that:

**a)** Is designated as "confidential," "proprietary," or similar marking; or

**b)** Should reasonably be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

### 2.2 Categories of Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:

- Business plans, strategies, and projections
- Customer lists, prospect data, and lead information
- Pricing, costs, and financial data
- Technical specifications and methodologies
- Software, algorithms, and AI systems
- Marketing materials and campaign strategies
- Employee and contractor information
- Trade secrets and proprietary processes
- Terms of this Agreement

### 2.3 Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that:

**a)** Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;

**b)** Was rightfully in the Receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure;

**c)** Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information;

**d)** Is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction;

**e)** Is disclosed with the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party; or

**f)** Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order, provided the Receiving Party gives prompt notice to the Disclosing Party to allow for protective measures.

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

### 3.1 Protection Standard

The Receiving Party agrees to:

**a)** Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;

**b)** Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;

**c)** Protect Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care;

**d)** Limit disclosure to employees, contractors, and advisors ("Representatives") who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as this Agreement;

**e)** Not copy or reproduce Confidential Information except as necessary for the Purpose;

**f)** Not reverse engineer, disassemble, or decompile any Confidential Information.

### 3.2 Responsibility for Representatives

The Receiving Party shall be responsible for any breach of this Agreement by its Representatives.

---

## 4. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION

### 4.1 Upon Request or Termination

Upon the Disclosing Party's written request or upon termination of this Agreement, the Receiving Party shall:

**a)** Promptly return all tangible Confidential Information to the Disclosing Party; or

**b)** Destroy all Confidential Information and certify such destruction in writing.

### 4.2 Exceptions

Notwithstanding the above, the Receiving Party may retain:

**a)** Copies required by law or regulation;

**b)** Archived copies in backup systems, provided such copies remain subject to confidentiality obligations.

---

## 5. NO LICENSE OR WARRANTY

### 5.1 No License Granted

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any license, right, or interest in the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information, intellectual property, or other proprietary rights.

### 5.2 No Warranty

ALL CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION IS PROVIDED "AS IS" WITHOUT WARRANTY OF ANY KIND, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, OR NON-INFRINGEMENT.

---

## 6. NO OBLIGATION

### 6.1 No Commitment

This Agreement does not obligate either Party to:

**a)** Enter into any business transaction;

**b)** Purchase or provide any products or services;

**c)** Disclose any particular Confidential Information.

### 6.2 Independent Development

Nothing in this Agreement shall prevent either Party from developing products, services, or concepts similar to those disclosed, provided such development does not use or derive from Confidential Information.

---

## 7. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 7.1 Term

This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **two (2) years** from the Effective Date, unless earlier terminated.

### 7.2 Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing thirty (30) days written notice to the other Party.

### 7.3 Survival

The confidentiality obligations under this Agreement shall survive termination for a period of **three (3) years** following such termination.

---

## 8. REMEDIES

### 8.1 Irreparable Harm

The Parties acknowledge that unauthorized disclosure of Confidential Information may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages may be inadequate.

### 8.2 Injunctive Relief

In the event of actual or threatened breach, the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief, specific performance, or other equitable remedies without the necessity of proving actual damages or posting bond.

### 8.3 Cumulative Remedies

Such equitable remedies shall be in addition to, and not in lieu of, any other remedies available at law or in equity.
