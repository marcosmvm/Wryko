## Competitive Response Playbook

### Scenario 1: Prospect Comparing to Enterprise Agency

**Situation:** "We're also talking to Belkins."

**Response:**
1. Acknowledge Belkins' reputation
2. Ask about their budget and timeline
3. Position cost-effectiveness: "If budget is flexible and you want the biggest brand, Belkins is solid. If you want similar results at 70% lower cost with faster onboarding, let's talk specifics."
4. Emphasize CSM automation as differentiator

---

### Scenario 2: Prospect Concerned About Our Size

**Situation:** "You're a newer/smaller company."

**Response:**
1. Acknowledge we're founder-led and growing
2. Emphasize automation reduces human error
3. Highlight performance guarantee (risk-free pilot)
4. Position Founding Partner benefits as incentive to try

---

### Scenario 3: Prospect Already Using DIY Tool

**Situation:** "We're using Apollo/Instantly already."

**Response:**
1. Ask about current results (reply rate, meetings)
2. Identify gaps in their current approach
3. Offer to audit their campaigns
4. Show how we'd improve with AI optimization
